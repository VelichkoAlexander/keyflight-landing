Have questions?

What is the flight reservation, how long it valid & etc.

  • What is the flight reservation?

    Flight reservation (also named itinerary, booking) – a document with detailed information about your flight. Such as flight name, departure/arrival timings along with dates, flight code, departure/arrival airport IATA codes and the flight reservation numbers. It may have PNR number, images, etc.

  • What is the PNR number?

    PNR also known as Passenger Name Record is a number with a unique 6 digit number, that is provided against every booked ticket. Whether you are booking the ticket online or physically (through ticket counters), the ticket will certainly have a PNR number.

  • What types of flight reservations do we have?

    We provide all types of flight reservations tickets such as sample, sample with PNR, verifiable with PNR.

  • What is the onward ticket?

    Onward ticket – ticket to one back way. Most often needed for border control. Also known as return ticket.

  • What is the sample ticket?

    Sample ticket (also named dummy) – example of the ticket. May look like verifiable ticket and have exactly the same information.

  • I need ticket for visa, which tariff should I buy?

    If you need ticket for visa centers you can use our tariff Reservation what includes flight itinerary with verifible PNR number and all detailed information about flight. Preferably order 24-30 hours before going to the embassy or visa center becouse of the limited valid ticket time. Usually reservation lasts 1-3 days (if the date of departure not close) and in rare cases 4-7 days. If the date of departure is close reservation can be valid less than 24 hours.
    Attention! Flight reservation can be valid maximum 7 days, but most frequently it’s 1 or 2 days.

  • I’m paid for the reservation, whats next?

    After your payment information about your order appears in our system. Usually, it takes 3-10 hours to process orders, but because of the difference in time zones, we recommend count down 24 hours. In the end, you will receive a letter that includes pdf-file with your flight itinerary.
    Attention ! We do not guarantee the urgent order fulfillment and strongly recommend place orders about 24-30 hours before you need a ticket.

  • Can I choose the airlines and what restrictions have you?

    Unfortunately, we can’t give the option of choosing an airline because not all airlines are initially available for booking flight reservations and many make it for a very short time, so we try to choose the most optimal company so the reservation would last as long as possible. In any other cases we don’t have restrictions and try to make best for you ❤︎

  • Whats the difference between sample ticket and flight itinerary?

    “Sample ticket” is just an example of the ticket. It can’t be checked and verified unlike the “real” flight itinerary which reserves you a place in a flight plane for a certain period. This itinerary can be checked on checkmytrip.com or airline website.

  • How the booking system works?

    After receiving payment, we begin to process the order. First, we select the best flights and send a booking request, we get information through the Global distribution system. If everything is ok with the flight we get a PNR code for issuing a ticket.

  • How long valid reservation?

    Reservation can be valid from few hours to maximum 7 days. It depends mostly on how far flight is and what airlines company flight we’ve booked. For example, you want to made reservation with flight tomorrow, so airline company can give you valid ticket, but after few hours or on next day your reservation will be closed becouse of interests of the airline company. They’ve close the reservation in order to be able to sell the latest flight tickets before boarding the flight. We try to keep valid the reservation as long as possible, therefore, we recommend that you do not take tickets for the coming dates (preferably at least 10 days later), because in this way you can reduce the service life of the reservation.

  • What if my reservation is cancelled?

    The validity of the flight reservation can be 7 days maximum. We try to keep valid the reservation as long as possible, therefore, we recommend that you do not take tickets for the coming dates (preferably at least 10 days later), because in this way you can reduce the service life of the reservation. If this situation happened to you we recommend to place new order with distant date.

  • How can I return money?

    Unfortunately, we work through a global distribution system and pay a commission for each reservation, so in order to make changes in it we need to book a new reservation, so the refund can’t be made if there was no reason, such as a mistake in the information about dates, names, etc. If such a mistake was made, please kindly make the new order or if you notice a mistake right after the payment writes us a letter and we will make an order with the right information.

  • How can I check my order?

    You can check your order via checkmytrip.com or airline website.
